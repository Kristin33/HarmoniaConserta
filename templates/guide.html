What is Harmonia Conserta?

Harmonia Conserta can complete a short four part harmony on a given key and harmonic progression. The harmonic writing will strictly follow the rules of eighteenth century voice leading procedures. This includes forbidding parallel/hidden/unequal/direct perfect intervals, overlapping/crossed voices, uncharacteristic leaps and so on and so on. Please do check it out and see how weird they are!

This project, of course, was inspired by the tedious harmonic free response question practices that I encountered when preparing for the AP music theory exam.

How to use it?

You can either input your own your harmony question, following the input instructions or see the demo questions from the past exams. Note that some 'uncharacteristic' inputs will result in no satisfactory answers:)
